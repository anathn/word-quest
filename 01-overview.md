# Word Quest: Spelling Adventure
## Game Design Document for 3rd Grade Spelling Practice

---

## 1. Game Overview

**Target Audience:** 3rd grade students (ages 8-9)
**Learning Objective:** Master spelling words through engaging gameplay with progress tracking
**Session Length:** 8-12 minutes per session
**Parent Setup:** Parent provides custom spelling word lists

### Core Concept
Word Quest is a space exploration game where students pilot a rocket ship through different planets. Each planet represents a spelling word challenge. Correctly spelling words fuels the rocket and helps it travel further through space. Missed words create "space debris" that must be cleared before continuing.

### Learning Principles
- **Active Practice:** Students must type/spell each word correctly to progress
- **Immediate Feedback:** Visual and audio cues within 1 second of response
- **Scaffolded Difficulty:** Words introduced in manageable chunks (2-3 letters at a time)
- **Mastery-Based Progression:** Must achieve 80% accuracy to unlock next level

---

## 2. Learning Objectives

By playing Word Quest, students will:
1. Memorize spelling word sequences through repeated practice
2. Develop letter recognition and sequencing skills
3. Build confidence in spelling through positive reinforcement
4. Track their own improvement over time
5. Transfer spelling skills from game to written assignments

### Alignment with 3rd Grade Standards
- Common Core L.3.2f: Spell grade-appropriate words correctly
- Develop phonics and word recognition skills
- Build vocabulary through word exposure
